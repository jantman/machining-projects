## 4. Notes on ISO Codes

- ISO codes listed as **probable** reflect standard industry mappings but are **not guaranteed**
- Exact insert size (e.g., 432 vs 431) often requires:
  - Measuring inscribed circle
  - Measuring thickness
  - Checking nose radius marking
- All **UNKNOWN** entries should be verified before re-ordering inserts
